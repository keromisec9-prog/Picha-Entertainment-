Filing a DMCA Notice

To file a valid DMCA notice, you must be the copyright owner or an authorized representative acting on their behalf. Please include the following information:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • Exact URL of the infringing material
  • Your contact information (name, email)
  • A statement of good faith belief
  • A statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury
  • Your full legal name (as signature)
  • Proof of Authorization if acting as a representative
